Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to register a company in Hong Kong?

+

With AIcountant, company registration typically takes 1-3 business days. Our streamlined digital process ensures quick processing once we receive all required documents.

What documents do I need to register a company?

+

You'll need: (1) Passport or HKID copy for all directors, (2) Proof of address, (3) Proposed company name, and (4) Business nature description.

Do I need a physical office in Hong Kong?

+

Yes, but it doesn't need to be a physical office you work from. We provide registered office address services starting from HK$199/month.

What taxes does a Hong Kong company pay?

+

Hong Kong has competitive rates: Profits Tax 8.25% (first HK$2M), then 16.5%. No VAT, no capital gains tax, no dividend tax.
